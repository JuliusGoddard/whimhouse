About Whim House

Wilding On A Whim

Glamping in Luxury and style, away from the stresses of the city.

Commissioned by royalty, Whim House was built in the year 2000, so that aristocrats could hunt away from the city. In 2026, Wilding on a Whim is a luxurious site of three glamping pods, a glass museum, meditation hut and music hut to give our guests a taste of the high life.

Come here alone, with your friends or family to live the life you have always deserved. Away from the pollution and hubbub of the cities, you can live like the aristocrats of old, enjoying nature, culture and comfort.

Wilding On A Whim

An immersive eco-retreat

Immerse yourself in our world, we offer not just three luxury glamping pods, but a meditation room, cinema with classical music, acres of untamed wildlife and all of this 30 minutes from the centre of Edinburgh.
